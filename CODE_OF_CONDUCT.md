# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

BitBasel is committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in this project — regardless of age, background, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

---

## Expected Behaviour

All participants are expected to:

- Communicate professionally and respectfully.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully and give it thoughtfully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community it serves.
- Show courtesy toward other contributors and maintainers.
- Respect differing viewpoints and approaches.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following are not tolerated in any project space, including the repository, issue tracker, pull requests, and any community channels:

- Harassment, intimidation, or threats of any kind.
- Discriminatory language or imagery.
- Personal attacks or insults directed at other participants.
- Publishing private information (physical or electronic) of others without explicit consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Reports of violations may be submitted to **conduct@bitbasel.miami**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may be removed from the project at the discretion of the core team.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and to public representations of the project. This includes use of project accounts on social media and representation at events.
